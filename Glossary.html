Glossary

  • Artificial Intelligence: AI is like a computer program that can think and learn like a human...
  • Authorisation: Authorization is like getting permission to do something...
  • Backup: A backup is a copy of all your computer's data, saved somewhere else...
  • Classified: Classified means something is given a specific label or category because...
  • Communication systems: These are methods or technology used to talk or share information...
  • Confidential: Confidential refers to information that must be kept private...
  • Cyber Criminals: These are people who use computers to commit crimes...
  • Cybersecurity: This is about protecting computers, networks, and data from being attacked...
  • Data: Data is any information that's stored or processed by a computer...
  • Data Breaches: This happens when private information is exposed or stolen...
  • Database: A database is like a digital filing cabinet where lots of information is stored...
  • Email: Email is a way to send messages and files over the internet...
  • Fraud: Fraud is when someone lies or deceives others to gain something valuable...
  • Hardware: Hardware is the physical part of a computer or any electronic system...
  • Identity theft: This is when someone steals your personal information to pretend to be you...
  • Insider Attacks: These are attacks that come from people within an organization...
  • Malware: Malware is software that's designed to harm or hack into computers and networks...
  • Microsoft: Microsoft is a large company that makes computer software and video games...
  • Phishing Attacks: This is when criminals send fake messages or emails trying to trick you...
  • Privacy: Privacy means keeping something secret or private, away from other people...
  • Rights: Rights are rules that protect people and allow them to do certain things freely...
  • Scamming: Scamming involves tricking someone into giving away their money or personal details...
  • Software: Software is a set of instructions that tells a computer how to operate...
  • Word: Definition of word.
