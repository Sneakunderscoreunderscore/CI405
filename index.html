Cybersecurity made fun

Who is this for?

This project is targeted towards children aged 11-14: However, we believe the information on this website will be useful for anybody interested in learning about cybersecurity and why it's so important to be aware of the risks that surround the internet.

What will you learn?

In the following informative webpages you will learn about creating and managing passwords, dangerous and missleading websites, and protecting your private information.

Why you should learn it?

Today we have the luxury to explore the vast expanse of the internet, but we sometimes forget about the dangers that follow. The information we teach in this website will hopefully help everybody be more aware of the dangers and allow users to navigate the internet more securly.
