Check your password:

Easy to guess passwords are a security risk.

The password checker below uses a personally designed JavaScript program which checks to see your passwords are valid and gives tips on how to make them stronger.

It will also use a real list of common passwords which a bad actor could use to attack your account. This is known as a dictionary attack and takes a brute force method.
